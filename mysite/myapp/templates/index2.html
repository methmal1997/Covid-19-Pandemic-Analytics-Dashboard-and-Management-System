Description

The corona virus pandemic which initially reported from China in late December in 2019, started spreading around the world so fast in the beginning of the 2020. The first corona virus infected person in Sri Lanka was reported on 27th January 2020.
The pandemic directly effects badly not only health but all areas, mainly economy and education in the country. Therefore, managing the spread of virus in the country is very essential. In order to face and manage the situation, the best decisions has to be made, so an occurrence of another wave can be prevented. Gathering all the necessary data and representing them and providing valuable information in one place would make the process of necessary decision making much easier. With Covid-19 Pandemic Analytic Dashboard and Management System,
our main aim is to provide that and being beneficial to many related parties not only the general public.
